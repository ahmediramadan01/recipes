Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of wheat flour
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Vegetable oil (to grease the pan)
  • Toppings to taste (maple syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, etc.)

Preparation:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the milk, egg and melted butter until you obtain a homogeneous mixture.
  3. Pour the liquid mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients.
  4. Stir gently with a spoon or whisk until the ingredients are just combined.
  5. Do not over mix as this can make the pancakes heavy.
  6. Preheat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  7. Lightly grease the frying pan with vegetable oil.
  8. Use about 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake.
  9. Pour the batter into the hot frying pan and spread it out slightly to form a round pancake.
  10. Cook until small bubbles appear on the surface of the pancake and the edges are lightly browned. This takes about 2-3 minutes.
  11. Flip the pancake with a spatula and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown all over.
  12. Transfer the pancake to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining dough.
  13. Serve the pancakes hot with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, etc.

Additional Information:

This recipe yields approximately 8 medium pancakes. You can adjust the size of the pancakes according to your preference. Enjoy your delicious American pancakes!
