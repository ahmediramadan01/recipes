Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (tea) of wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoon of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 spoons cornstarch
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 4 tablespoons melted unsalted butter
  • 1 and 3/4 cup (tea) milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla essence

Preparation:

  1. Sift the wheat flour, sugar, starch, yeast and salt into a container.
  2. In another container, mix the beaten eggs with the milk, melted butter and vanilla essence.
  3. Pour over the flour mixture and quickly incorporate the ingredients.
  4. Heat the waffle iron.
  5. Place a shallow ladle of dough and spread it until it covers the device mold, close the lid and let it bake until the dough is golden brown.
  6. Remove with silicone spatulas.
  7. Serve with honey, fruit or jam.

Additional Information:

Suitable for eating with friends in the morning :D
