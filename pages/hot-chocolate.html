Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup chocolate powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A bit of salt
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Grated chocolate (optional)

Preparation:

  1. In a pan, heat the milk over medium heat until it starts to boil.
  2. Stir occasionally to prevent the milk from sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  3. Add the chocolate powder, sugar and salt to the hot milk.
  4. Stir well until the chocolate and sugar are completely dissolved.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the hot chocolate cook for another 2 to 3 minutes, stirring constantly. This will help thicken the drink slightly.
  6. Remove the pan from the heat and add the vanilla extract. Mix well.
  7. Pour the hot chocolate into cups or mugs. If desired, add whipped cream on top to decorate and sprinkle with grated chocolate.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious hot chocolate!

Additional Information:

Remember that you can adjust the amount of sugar to suit your personal taste. Additionally, you can also add other ingredients, such as ground cinnamon or marshmallows, to give your hot chocolate an extra kick.
